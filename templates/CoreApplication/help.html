Help - Support Center

1. Getting Started

How to create an account?

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page. Fill out the form with your email address and a password. Then, confirm your registration by clicking the link sent to your email.

How to log in?

Click on the "Login" button in the top right corner of the page. Enter your email address and password, then click "Login."

2. Uploading and Working with Files

How to upload a file?

On the homepage, you can upload a file by clicking the "Choose File" button. Select the file from your computer and click "Submit." Supported file formats are listed on the upload page.

How to check the upload status?

After uploading a file, the processing status will be displayed on the screen. If the file is successfully uploaded, you will see a confirmation message.

3. Working with the RSS News Feed

How to view the RSS feed?

The RSS news feed is available on the right side of the page. You can scroll through the feed to view the latest news. Click on a news headline to read the full article.

What to do if the news feed doesn't load?

If the news feed doesn't load, try refreshing the page. Ensure that your internet connection is stable. If the problem persists, please contact our support team.

4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to change my password?

To change your password, log into your account and go to the "Settings" section. Under "Security," select "Change Password." Enter a new password and save the changes.

What to do if I forgot my password?

If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we will send you instructions to reset your password.
